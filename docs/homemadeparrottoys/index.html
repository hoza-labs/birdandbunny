Homemade Parrot Toys
Cheap, easy, and fun!
Toys are one of the most important factors in keeping a healthy, happy pet. Yes, I said healthy. Especially with birds, toys help prevent issues that boredom can induce, such as the common problem of feather plucking. However, toys are expensive, and owners may be dismayed to find that their pet has efficiently destroyed a $20 toy within two hours. Some owners simply can't afford to spend money on the toys their birds (or other pets) need for mental stimulation. That's why people make their own toys. They're cheap, easy, and fun, too!
To make the toys on this site, all you need is some bird-safe rope, cardboard, and paper.
