Tossing Toy
MATERIALS: cardboard, scissors, paper/rope
1. Cut a piece of cardboard about 2" x 2". Then cut 3 holes in a diagonal line accross the square.
2. Cut either 3 strips of paper or rope. I prefer paper because it makes the toy lighter and easier to throw and around and destroy, but rope is good if you want the toy to last longer. If you use paper, scrunch and twist the pieces up so they can fit through the holes.
2. Poke the pieces of paper or rope through the holes. If you use rope, tie knots on either side of the cardboard or around the sides for the end pieces.
